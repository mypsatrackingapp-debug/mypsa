# Privacy Policy — MyPSA

Last updated: April 2025

## Overview
MyPSA is a personal health tracking application for monitoring PSA (Prostate-Specific Antigen) levels. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect
- **Account Information:** Email address and name, used to create and manage your account.
- **Health Data:** PSA test results and related medical data you enter manually.

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide the app core functionality (storing and displaying your PSA history).
- To calculate personalised risk assessments based on EAU/AUA 2024 guidelines.
- We do **not** sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

## Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud infrastructure, protected with encryption in transit and at rest.

## Data Retention
Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request full deletion at any time.

## Your Rights
You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
